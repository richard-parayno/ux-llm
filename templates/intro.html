Let's learn what
intavue.ai is capable of

Interviewing requires a human touch. That's why Intavue.ai is an assistant in the interview process and doesn't replace the role of the interviewer. The tool requires a research goal, research questions, research hypotheses, and an interview transcript from the user for it to work.

{% include 'icon_do.html' %}

DO

  • Use the provided Research Goals, Research Questions, and Research Hypotheses
  • Completely fill up all input fields before proceeding
  • Share your screen to the Intavue team while using this tool
  • Use the created outputs of the system if you find it useful
{% include 'icon_dont.html' %}

DON'T

  • Change the content of the provided Research Goals, Research Questions, and Research Hypotheses
  • Proceed without filling up all the input fields
  • Use the tool without sharing your screen to the Intavue team
  • Use the created outputs of the system if you don't find it useful
